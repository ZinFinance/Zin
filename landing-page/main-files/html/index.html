Buy back & Burn with Staking

Zin will be an intuitive, simple to use Investment platform which will combine the investment tools of crypto and traditional stocks. Its fantastic UI will be focused towards building a growth nest, rather than trading.

Zin will be a 100% deflationary token. The token holders will be able to earn more Zin through staking and enjoy the appreciation with our profit sharing buyback & burn until all supply is burned. Zin profits will come from the usage of its investment platform.
